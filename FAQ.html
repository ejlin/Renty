Frequently Asked Questions:


Q1: What is Renty?

A1: Renty aims to reduce consumer cost and save time and hassle.Renty allows people to rent out items lying around that other people have a use for. This way, the owner can make some quick money while the renter can save money on an item they only have a sparing need for.


Q2: When/why was Renty founded?

A2: Renty is the brainchild of a project for SDHacks, a local hackathon taking place at UCSD. It's a fun project (right now) that will hopefully turn into something more.


Q3: Does Renty provide a guarantee for renter's as of now?

A3: Renty will provide assurance for all renter's to ensure their comfortability and guarantee that renters are at no risk through monetary compensation.


Q4: How does the transfer of items work?

A4: Renty will pay for the shipping cost for sending items to and from buyers for items rented over $25. Individual parties will be responsible for shipping costs for items under $25. For items located nearby and reachable by hand delivery, we will reimburse the driver up to 15 cents per mile driven.
